3D Game of Life

A three-dimensional representation of Conway's Game of Life. Features both a flying and walking camera perspective. The final goal of the project was to have a world generate around you in accordance with the rules of the game of life which you would then have to navigate in order to reach some goal. There are no stages yet, but the framework is in place

Wavelet Module

Developed for Hendrix College Physics Department

This module was designed to read in data from ring lasers that had been counted by WaveMetrics Igor. Data can be broken down into Haar wavelets of any size, up to the largest power of 2 smaller than the input dataset size.
